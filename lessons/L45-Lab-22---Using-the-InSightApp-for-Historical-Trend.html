Lab 22 – Using the InSightApp for Historical

Trending

Introduction

In this lab, you will use the InSightApp to add a display for historical trending in your ViewApp. You

will configure the InSightApp to retrieve relevant information from Historian.

When testing, you will be able to use different tools to view data and alarm-related information

through the InSightApp.

For example, you will be able to zoom, pan, and extend the trend display based on the time range.

You will be able to move your mouse over the trend line to view data based on the cursor position

on the line, and click the highlighted part of the line to view alarm information. You will also be able

to view the data in different units of measure.

Objectives

Upon completion of this lab, you will be able to:



Configure the InSightApp



Use the InSightApp for historical trending
