Lab 19 – Creating a Live Alarm Display and Managing Alarms at Runtime

Lab 19 – Creating a Live Alarm Display and

Managing Alarms at Runtime

Introduction

In this lab, you will use the AlarmApp to create a display for current alarms.

You will create a new layout named LiveAlarms and associate the AlarmApp to it. You will

configure the AlarmApp from within the Layout Editor, to create alarm queries and filters and

customize its appearance and behaviors.

Then in the ViewApp Editor, you will add the LiveAlarms layout to a pane and associate the Plant

navigation item to it.

When testing in a preview session, you will use the AlarmApp to perform some alarm management

tasks, such as viewing alarms from different areas in your plant and navigating to the asset for a

selected alarm within your ViewApp. Additional tasks include acknowledging alarms, shelving and

unshelving alarms, and hiding and unhiding alarms from the alarm display.

Content workflow highlight: Configuring a pane’s presentation style to allow support of multiple

content items

Objectives

Upon completion of this lab, you will be able to:



Configure the AlarmApp to display current alarms



Create default alarm queries for the AlarmApp



Create and manage additional queries and filters for the AlarmApp



Customize appearance and behavior configurations for the AlarmApp



Acknowledge alarms through the AlarmApp in runtime



Hide and unhide alarms in runtime



Shelve and unshelve alarms through the AlarmApp in runtime
