Lab 13 – Adding a Home Carousel

Introduction

In this lab, you will add the Carousel widget to your ViewApp. You will create a layout, assign the

Carousel widget to a pane, and configure the widget to display a set of Overview graphics.

Then you will assign the new layout to the Home navigation item in the ViewApp Editor and

configure AutoFill settings.

When you test your ViewApp in the preview session, you will navigate to Home and see the

Carousel widget cycle through a display of Overview graphics.

Objectives

Upon completion of this lab, you will be able to:



Use the Carousel widget in a ViewApp



Configure a Home display in a ViewApp
